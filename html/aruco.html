ArUco markers on Unity

List of small project using ArUco on Unity to create simple augmented reality application. Documentation can be found here.

Volume control

Control the volume of a music based on the distance between two markers. The closer they are, the louder the music is.

Calculator

Calculate the resul of the multiplication of the yellow number and the red number and show it (green number). To incremente you can tap on the marker or hide the number. You can reset the state by taping on the green one.

Laser

Different type of element are attach to markers:

  • A laser projector, project a laser in front of it, the orientation can be changed by moving the marker
  • A cube that block the laser when it touch it
  • A light that turn ON when the laser go through it
  • A mirror that reflect the laser
In this project we can see that this implementation is very dependent on the quality of the camera and the light of the room. The marker also need to be printed on a very flat surface. All the condition aren't respected in my room so this can explain why the markers are moving like that.
